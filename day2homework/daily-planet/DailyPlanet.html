Refugees Flood Across Europe

Eurozone governments are reporting that the amassing of Syrian refugees on their borders has reached levels unseen since the end of World War II. The leaders of Greece, Macedonia, Cyprus and Croatia have appealed to the EU for more open immigration policies and greater aid in order to accomodate refugees fleeing violence in Syria and Iraq.

Merkel Pleads for Calm

German chancellor Angela Merkel appealed to European Union leaders for more open borders, citing the unprecedented flow of migrants toward Germany. Addressing fences being placed along Hungary and Croatia, Merkel requested that EU member states uphold their commitments to open borders.
